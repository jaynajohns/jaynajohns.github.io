Born on July 12 in the greatish state of Texas, I would become the loudest quiet person ever. I stood out from my brothers and sister in that I didn't stand out. You could forget about me; you couldn't forget about them. But when I speak, I'm heard...usually. Even the quietest raised in such a boisterous group can't help but pick up some of their characteristics - and the enigma that is the quiet girl who won't stop trying to talk over people will forever baffle and amuse - and maybe occasionally frustrate - those around me. This will become a brief, professional autobiography when inspiration properly strikes.
